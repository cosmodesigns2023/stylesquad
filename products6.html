Color analysis for men, also known as men's color analysis or personal color analysis for men, is a process that helps individuals determine which colors best complement their natural skin tone, hair color, and eye color. This analysis is typically done to enhance one's personal style, clothing choices, and overall appearance. Here are some details and descriptions of package commonly offered in men's color analysis services.

  • The first step in color analysis is identifying your skin tone. Men typically fall into one of the following categories: warm, cool, or neutral. This is determined by the undertones in your skin.
  • Color draping involves placing various fabric swatches of different colors near your face to see how they affect your skin tone. The colors that make your complexion appear healthy, bright, and harmonious are typically your best choices.
  • Based on the draping results, you'll be categorized into a seasonal color palette. Common palettes include Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter.
  • Once you know your color palette, you can choose clothing in those colors. These colors can be used for shirts, ties, suits, and accessories.
