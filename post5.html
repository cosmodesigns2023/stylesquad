Summer vs autumn colour analysis how to identify your old colour?


To determine whether you have a summer or autumn color palette, consider the undertones of your skin, hair, and eye color. Summer individuals typically have cooler undertones with fair to light skin, as well as light-colored eyes and ashy or cool-toned hair. In contrast, autumn individuals lean towards warmer undertones, featuring medium to dark skin, rich eye colors, and hair with warm or golden undertones. Additionally, summer shades complement pastel hues and cool-toned neutrals, while autumn shades are harmonious with earthy, warm colors like deep reds, oranges, and olive greens. Identifying your undertones and preferred color range can help you determine your ideal color palette for clothing and makeup.


  • Cool Undertones:
    Summers typically have cool undertones in their skin, hair, and eyes. This means their veins appear bluish, and their skin has a pink or rosy undertone.

  • Best Colors:
    Summers look fantastic in cool, soft, and muted colors. Think pastels, cool blues, soft pinks, lavenders, and grays. These shades harmonize with their cool undertones and enhance their natural beauty.

  • Warm Undertones:
    Autumns have warm undertones in their skin, hair, and eyes. Their veins may appear more greenish, and their skin often has a golden or peachy undertone.

  • Flexibility and Adaptation:
    While consistency is essential, brands may adapt their color palette slightly for specific campaigns or seasons. This allows for flexibility while maintaining overall brand recognition.

  • Avoid:
    Summers should generally avoid overly bright or warm colors like bright oranges, yellows, or warm reds, as these can make them appear washed out or sallow.


    To identify your color season more accurately, consider consulting a professional color analyst or taking an online quiz designed to determine your seasonal color palette. Keep in mind that individual variations exist, and these descriptions provide a general guideline to help you discover the colors that enhance your natural beauty the most.
